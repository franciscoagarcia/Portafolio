## Descripción

**_Aurora_** is a logistics system that allows you to dispatch goods quickly through a simple experience and provides dynamic tracking until they reach their destination. Developed on the .NET 8.0 platform, MySQL, and Dapper as a micro ORM for database management and administration. **_Aurora_** achieves clean and structured code ready for future evolution.
